About

Lightning Labs offers 25ID a mission driven approach to Innovation and Modernization. We provide Soldiers with a physical makerspace and digital platforms for creative design and collaboration aimed at problems identified by Soldiers in their individual mission sets and capability gaps identified by command. Lightning Labs also provides consolidated and ongoing support via access to small grants, patent agents, workshops, and connections to 25ID senior leaders and academic partners. As the primary innovation hub for jungle and nearshore warfare, Lightning Labs fosters innovation at the lowest level of command ensuring our division's readiness for the challenges of tomorrow's USINDOPACOM theater. We believe that great ideas can come from anyone, anywhere, irrespective of rank or position.
